My goals for today's talk

  • (Re-)brand OGGM as a modelling framework in which you can fit your own model and ideas
  • Demonstrate some of OGGM's features, including modularity, online tutorials and reproducible science
  • Discuss the challenges the project is facing ...
  • ... and propose ways forward
    (which may involve your participation)

Today's Non-goals

  • Sell OGGM as "the model which has it all":
    other models out there are objectively "better", and model diversity is the motto that drives our project
  • Getting lost in project specific details: the ideas and tools presented here are applicable to all
